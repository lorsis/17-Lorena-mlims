Seaside farmlands

Cherry

Origin

It has its origin in Asia minor, the tree was introduced by the Romans who selected and improved their cultivation, reaching its maximum development in this area, in the Arab era.

Where to find them

Flowering period: Usually starts from the second half of March, although it can vary depending on the place, the rainfall or the temperatures of each season. All these factors also influence whether flowering is more explosive or progressive. The most varieties of flower colors range from white, light yellow, pink or a subtle mix. The color of some varieties may change during the flowering period. The flower usually stays in the tree for about 15 days. During that period it is spectacular to see the mantle of flowers that covers the valleys and you can observe and enjoy from any corner of the roads and paths that run them.

Collection period:The approximate collection date is 15 April until the end of July, although this date varies as it depends on weather circumstances.
