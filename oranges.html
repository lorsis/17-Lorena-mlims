Oranges

Origin

Oranges and tangerines are two varieties of citrus, their origin comes from Asia, were introduced into the Iberian Peninsula by the Arabs in the 10th century, was a bitter modality, in the XV came from the hands of the Portuguese new varieties with a much sweeter and flavorful taste: citrus sinensis (orange) and citrus reticulata (orange tangerine). Interestingly, that’s where the name comes from of 'oranges of China', since its origin was precisely the ancient Asian country.

Where to find them

The Valencian Community is the main citrus region at national level, both for the area dedicated to this crop as by production. In the Marina Alta predominates above all in the northern area in municipalities such as: Dénia, Els Poblets, El Verger, Beniarbeig, Ondara, Pego, Pedreguer and the so-called peoples of Retoria.

Experiencias

Flowering period: It is in spring when the orange tree is covered with orange blossoms that impregnate the landscape with a pleasant aroma. Most of the flowers fall to the ground when opened, forming a mantle white around the tree. The other flowers, which have been pollinated, turn into oranges, through the so-called curdled fruit which is the passage from flower to fruit, although we will have to wait a few months before they reach our table. The maturation phase extends from July to February. During this period the growth and fattening of most varieties of oranges.

Harvesting period: The orange season lasts practically nine months of the year. This does not mean that a tree is giving oranges all this time, there are different varieties of oranges, and each of them has its own months of collection. In this way we find oranges from October or November to the end of June or July. These dates are not accurate nor constant weather, and specifically rainfall and temperature (and to a lesser extent wind) have much to say in this regard, as well as where the plantation is located. So both, the beginning or end of the season of oranges can vary depending on the peculiarities that occur each year in particular.
